## Filled Example: Website Redesign

### Cover

**Website Redesign & Conversion Optimization**
Prepared for: Sarah at GreenLeaf Consulting
Prepared by: Mitch / Speakeasy
Date: February 20, 2026

### Executive Summary

Your current site gets traffic but doesn't convert — visitors leave before booking a call. We'll redesign your site around your sales process, add a clear booking funnel, and optimize for mobile. Goal: double your booked-call rate within 90 days of launch.

### Scope of Work

| Deliverable | Description | Timeline |
|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Site Audit | Full audit of current site: analytics, UX, conversion points | Week 1 |
| Wireframes | Page-by-page wireframes for 5 core pages | Week 2 |
| Design & Build | Custom design and development on Framer | Week 3-5 |
| Booking Funnel | Calendly integration with qualification form | Week 5 |
| Launch & QA | Cross-browser testing, mobile optimization, DNS cutover | Week 6 |
| Post-Launch Support | Bug fixes and minor tweaks for 2 weeks after launch | Week 7-8 |

### Pricing

**Tier 1 — Core: $4,500**
- Site audit, wireframes, design & build (5 pages), launch

**Tier 2 — Standard: $6,500** (Recommended)
- Everything in Core, plus:
  - Booking funnel with qualification form
  - SEO setup (meta tags, sitemap, schema)
  - Analytics dashboard (GA4 + goals)

**Tier 3 — Premium: $9,000**
- Everything in Standard, plus:
  - Copywriting for all 5 pages
  - 3 months of monthly performance reports
  - Priority support channel

### Payment Terms
- 50% ($3,250 for Standard) upon signing
- 50% upon launch

### Next Steps
1. Pick your tier
2. Reply "let's go" or schedule a 15-min call to discuss
3. First payment and kickoff within 48 hours
